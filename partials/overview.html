Group Management

Creating a group allows for additional features that you won't get otherwise

Privacy

A group can be created with one of three levels of privacy. This controls how much open your group is to the public.

  • Public: Users can search and join your group freely.
  • Semi-Private: Users can search for your group, but must request and receive permission to join.
  • Private: The group is hidden and users can only join via email invitation.
Administration

A group can only be owned by a single user but other members can be made administrators.

Admins have extended privelidges that allow them to create events, accept new members, and send group emails.

Events that are created can have a max capacity, so its easy to limit the number of people who sign up.

E-Mail

Group admins may send emails to all members of the group. No need to keep a separate email list.

Users may subscribe to email alerts on events. Easily being notified of any changes or updates.
